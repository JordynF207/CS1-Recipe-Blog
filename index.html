üç´Chocolate CakeüéÇ

Description

This recipe is home cooked delicous chocolate cake, it is the perecet desert and the best way to brighten your day!

Ingredients

  • All purpose flower
  • Granulated suagr
  • Brown sugar
  • Pure vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder
  • Two eggs
  • Sour cream
  • Oil
  • Hot coffee
  • Dark chocolate
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingrients: sift flower, cocoa, baking powder and baking soda all into a large bowl
  2. Blend in sugar. Add both sugars and salt and whisk to blend well, pressing out any lumps of brown sugar.
  3. Combine wet ingredients. Combine eggs, oil, sour cream and vanilla in a medium bowl and whisk to blend well, then add to the dry ingredients. In a pinch you can also add these ingredients directly to the bowl with the dry ingredients.
  4. Mix wet and dry ingredients. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients and mix with a whisk or an electric hand mixer on medium-low until blended. It will be thick and somewhat dry.
  5. Mix in hot coffee. Add the hot coffee gradually in two stages to minimize clumps forming and beat until evenly combined and the batter smooth.
  6. Bake. Divide batter evenly between the prepared pans and bake for 30-33 minutes until cakes spring back when pressed gently and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Transfer pans to a wire rack and let cool for 15 minutes before inverting onto the rack to cool completely.

Scrape down the sides and up the bottom of the bowl with a silicone spatula as needed. Add the oil and beat for 1 minute until combined.   6. Allow cakes to cool in the pans set on a wire rack for 20 minutes. Run a knife around the edges and then remove cakes from pans. Place cakes directly on wire racks to cool completely. The cakes must be completely cool before frosting and assembling.

Run a bench scraper around the cake to smooth out crumb coat. Chill uncovered in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes and up to 3 hours to set the crumb coat.
  9. Spread the remaining frosting all over the top and sides. Garnish with extra sprinkles, if desired.
